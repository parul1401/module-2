OUR MENU

Chicken

When you are trying to eat better and/or lose weight chicken is a popular choice whether you are cooking for yourself or your entire family. Ever versatile, it can be prepared so many different ways.

Beef

There should be no excessive fat. The lean meat should be flecked with fat which is known as marbling. The fat should be dry, creamy white in colour and odourless.

Sushi

Take that sashimi and place it on a little pillow of rice and you have nigiri sushi. The rice is gently flavored with vinegar, and each piece of sushi should amount to a single bite, according to Hiyakawa.
